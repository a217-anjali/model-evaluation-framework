## Purpose

Public-facing services must meet all 14 points of the Service Standard and pass a service assessment. Services processing over 100,000 transactions/year require cross-government panel assessment.

## Assessment Process

### Assessment format
- 4-hour assessment with panel
- 10-minute service overview presentation
- 20-minute user journey walkthrough
- Remaining time: panel Q&A

### Panel composition
- Lead assessor
- User researcher
- Designer
- Technical lead
- Performance analyst (sometimes)

### Assessment ratings
- **Green** — Meets standard, proceed
- **Amber** — Non-critical issues, proceed but address within 3 months
- **Red** — Critical issues, remain in current phase

### AI-specific areas panels will probe

- [ ] How does the AI address genuine user needs? (not technology-push)
- [ ] What happens when users are not eligible or AI cannot process their case?
- [ ] What offline channels and human fallback processes exist?
- [ ] Does the service work for all users including edge cases?
- [ ] How is AI performance measured and published?

## Output

Service assessment result (Green / Amber / Red) with action items.
