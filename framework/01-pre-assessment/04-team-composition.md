## Purpose

Every UK framework emphasises that AI/LLM projects must not be run by technologists alone. Multidisciplinary teams with diverse expertise are required to identify and mitigate risks that a purely technical team would miss.

## AI Playbook Learner Groups

The AI Playbook identifies five groups who need AI capability:

1. **Beginners** — Basic AI literacy
2. **Technical non-digital** — Domain professionals who interact with AI outputs
3. **Data and analytics** — Staff who work with AI data
4. **Digital professionals** — Engineers and developers building AI systems
5. **Leaders** — Decision-makers who authorise and govern AI use

## Output

A team composition document showing:
1. Named individuals in each required role
2. Gaps in capability and plan to fill them
3. Training needs across the five learner groups
